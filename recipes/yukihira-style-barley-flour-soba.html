Yukihira Style Barley Flour Soba

Description

Yukihira Style Barley Flour Yaki Soba is a dish made by Sōma Yukihira against Nene Kinokuni during the 3rd Card of the 1st Bout of the Rebels Vs. Central Régiment de Cuisine. Through the usage of thrice milled buckwheat flour, the delicate flavor of Soba remains preserved despite frying it. Eating the dish invokes nostalgic summer festival memories of one's youth.

Ingredients

Soba

  • 8 Parts Unrefined Thrice-milled Buckwheat Flour
  • 2 Parts Barley Flour
  • Water

Sauce

  • Dashi
  • Kaeshi
    • 10 Parts Dark Soy Sauce
    • 4 Parts Mirin
    • 3 Parts Brown Sugar

Stir-Fry

  • Duck Breast (Thinly Sliced)
  • Scallions (Fried)
    • Duck Fat

Condiments

  • Sesame Seeds (Ground & Toasted)
  • Sichuan Peppers
  • Ginger

Garnish

  • Scallion (Shredded)

Steps

  1. Combine all your sauce ingredients together
  2. Prep your veggies and cook your noodles.
    Note: some varieties come precooked and only need a quick reheating.
  3. Cook all your veggies in a skillet until golden brown.
  4. Cook your chicken in a separate pan.
  5. Add your veggies back into your chicken pan. Add in the sauce and stir to combine.
  6. Add your noodles into the mixture. Cook for a few minutes. Serve!
